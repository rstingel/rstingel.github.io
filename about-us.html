About Us

We started out as a couple of friends who want the world not to be scammed by car sales. We hold a promise to be fair and adequate.

Even though we may seem like other "car salesmen", we have a promise; our reviews say so.

We may have limited stock; the cars may be old and mere unusable. If you have a problem within 72 hours of purchase, we will refund the car and repair it. (It cannot have been on purpose, for instance, a wreck. The things we do accept are, engine failure or any other critical component failure. If it's evident that the vehicle has been tampered with, the vehicle warranty will be void.)
